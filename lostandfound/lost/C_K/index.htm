~ Cocks & Kulick G/Gas Anglia ~

 
 

You have a great site, I would like to submit 2 pictures of our Anglia with the hope that we could find out whatever happened to it. If you could put it into "Lost and Found" we would appreciate it.

The car was owned by Arthur Cocks and Ed Kulick, run in the 70's around the New York area (Islip Speedway, Westhampton, National Raceway). It was powered by a 301 c.i. GMC 6 cylinder, ran G/Gas, was a crowd pleaser and knocked quite a few bad V-8's out of competition. We were sponsored by C&A Speed in St. James, Long Island.  We sold it in the early 80's and heard it was campaigned with a small block V8 but that's all we know. Please let me know if you have any information.
