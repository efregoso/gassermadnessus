~ Bob Mehler's "Dirty Ole Man" 55 Chevy Gasser ~

 
  Wanted Dead Or Alive! Looking for info on this car:

1955 Chevy Gasser - "Dirty Ole Man"
Sold in early '74 and think it went to B.C. Canada. Orange with white stripes, ran 331" Hilborn Injected small block chevy engine (set back 10%), 5.86 Pontiac Rear, T-10 4-speed stick, unique front straight axle car with transverse leaf setup. Interior had the "dog house" trap door to get to the engine. All steel front (removeable). Car raced from '66 or so through 1973 at Puyallup, Shelton, Bremerton, Arlington, Pacific Raceways. Any info on the whereabouts of the car or maybe who owned it since it was sold in '74 would be greatly appreciated! See the ad attached from The National Dragster Sept. 14, 1973. Reward for right info leading to the car. Thanks!
