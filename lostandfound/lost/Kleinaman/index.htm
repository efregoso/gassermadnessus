Byron,
I was just told about your site and checked it out. I was wanting to post a few pics in your lost and found area. My dad and I are trying to locate is old dragcar. It ran at US 30 Dragstrip in the 60s and 70s. Here are some pictures of the car. It was a 1964 Dodge Dart. The car had a lot of changes made to it. As you can see from the pictures the driver sat in the back seat, roof was cut off at the quarter pillars, the engine was moved back to were the front seat would be and had a vinyl cover snap on cover over it . It had a chromemoly straight front axle under it. This car was ran almost every weekend at US 30 Dragstrip in Indiana. The car was also street legal (lights all worked, had pipes to put on it, and had to wear goggle when driving) and was used on the street when they were not at the track. He ran this car from the mid 60's until the mid 70's before selling it. At the time he sold it he was running a 440 in it. We are trying to locate the car again, or see if anyone has any pics of it at the track running. The pictures I sent you are all we have of the car now. the car ran in the yellow and black color scheme first and in the 70's it went to the blue and black color scheme
