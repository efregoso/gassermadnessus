~ Dave Heston's 39 Chevy Gasser "Warhammer"~

Just finished ’39 Chevy Vietnam Veteran’s tribute car, "Warhammer". 383 Stroker, 500 HP 11:1, Dart Heads built by Outlaw Racing Engines in Camdenton, MO, final tuning by Rick Pope Automotive – Lubbock, TX. TCI Streetfighter 3800 stall, Ford 9” Posi w/4:86 Gears, Chris Alston rear coilovers, suspension by Chris Bacciocco – Sonoma, CA. Immoral support from Mick Coyne J Show donations will be made to the Fisher House, a veteran’s support organization.
Dave Heston
