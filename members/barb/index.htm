~ Barb Hamilton-Advey ~

As the owner-builder-driver of this C/GS Willys, Barbara Hamilton, Hall of Fame member and the first woman ever licensed by NHRA to drive a supercharged car, terrorized first, Midwest and then Southern California dragstrips (and the NHRA Record) for a number of years. The car resides in the Garlits museum.  In 2001 she decided she needed a street rod too, so...what would you guess...of course it's a 40 Willys with a blown, injected small block Chevy...what else?
