~ Rest in Peace ~

~ Ron Miller ~ (1939-2010)

Ron Miller was my friend.  Of course anyone that ever met Ron would tell you the same thing.  He was one of those rare individuals who made everyone feel as if they'd been friends with him forever.

Ron was a member of the Winslow Drag Racing Hall of Fame for an extensive record of event wins, class wins and a number of points championships over a 50 year drag racing career in Arizona, California and New Mexico.

All that aside, Ron is missed by everyone who knew him...everyone who could say "Ron Miller was my friend"!

Vaya con Dios, Ron.
