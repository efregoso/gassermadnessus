~ Rest in Peace ~

~ Tim Hanaseth ~

Tim Hanaseth lost his battle with cancer on his birthday, 22 February 2010.

If you've been visiting Gasser Madness for a while, you've probably seen hundreds of his photos from the past several years at the March Meets and California Hot Rod Reunions.  Tim's been shooting great pix for Gasser Madness since we first met in 2001.

Tim really loved going out and shooting photos at the track.  He was a great guy and will be greatly missed by those who knew him.

I'll sure miss him. Vaya con Dios, Tim.
