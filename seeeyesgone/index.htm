Byron
Your site is great ! I am restoring the 40 Willys coupe called "See Eyes Gone " you have pictured on your site. It won the Nascar Winter Nationals and was a great wheelstander in its day. I have met with Gerald Nix the original builder and are putting it back to its gasser glory and plan on racing it. I thought I would send you some of the pictures and newspaper clippings Gerald has given me. He has been a great help and is excited about the project. I think you will get a kick out of the pictures.

Thanks, Mike Frank
