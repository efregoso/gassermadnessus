Date: June 28, 2008 9am-11pm
Location: National Trail Raceway, Hebron, OH

***Drag Racing***
-Nostalgic "Flag Style" Drags with a flagger, actual racing will be ET-style with handicaps
-Open to all pre-1972 Cars and all Traditional style Bikes (who can pass basic tech inspection)
-Slick Tire and Street Tire Classes
-Trophies will be awarded for street tire class
-Purses will be awarded for slick tire class (1st= $500, 2nd= $200, Semis=$100)
-Time slips to all participants
-tech inspection starts at 9am

***Car/Bike Show***
-Open to pre-1972 American cars and Traditional Style and custom motorcycles of all makes and models. No Sportbikes or off-road bikes/atvs.
-Trophies/awards for top 3 Road Devils Favorites

***Burn-Out Contest***
-will take place in water box area
-Trophy/award awarded for Crowd favorite

***Flame-Thrower Contest***
-Trophy/award for Crowd Favorite

***Pin-up contest**
-Ladies only......as if that needed to be mentioned....
-No official entry required, announcement will be made asking for participants
-trophy/award for crowd favorite

***Concert***
-6-10pm
-4 Bands
-Struttin' Cocks, Shotgun Kelly, Rumble Club
-Headliner: Corpse Show Creeps

***Vending***
-Free Vending with purchase of general admission for each person affiliated with the vending.
-No designated spots, however there may be a designated area, there will be more than enough room for vending
-NO FOOD OR DRINK VENDING!

***Beer***
-Beer will be available
-Participants in the racing may not drink until eliminated

Date/Time: 6/28/2008, 9am-11pm
Location: National Trail Raceway- 2650 National Rd. SW, Hebron, OH 43025
Admission: $15ea - Kids 12 and Under free.

Other Details:
-Basic Rules and info will be posted on official website
-Hotel accommodations/locations available on official website
-Contact info on official website
-Sponsorship welcome!!!!

The other being, The Muscle Car Reunion and Nostalgia Drags in Kansas City, MO. Also on tap are some good old Saturday night match races at the new Muncie Dragway and Route 66 Raceway.
