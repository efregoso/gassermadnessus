~ Ed Earl's 56 Chevy ~

Gassermadness viewers - another tri-5 Chevy from with Chicago area roots gets a restoration. I was able to find this one just 1 suburb over behind a garage where is sat under a cover for years. The owner was an old gasser racer in the days. He ran the straight axle 56 Chevy named Hombre. Unfortunately this isn't that car. But it has all the old school goodies - 60 olds rear end with the big honkin homemade ladder bars, glass front end, straight axle, Eelco tank in the front and fenderwell headers.

Ed Earl
