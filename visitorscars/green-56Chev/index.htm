Just a recent pic of my 56 I sent in a few years back. Thanks for the wonderful site!
Dustin Green

Hi. My name is Dustin and I am 18 years old from West Virginia. I love the 60's gasser look of the tri-5 Chevys and decided to build my own about a year ago. I have it set up for a .040 over 440 Mopar engine with 727 trans and 83/4 rear hosing a 4:10 posit unit. I have 36 inch ladder bars installed and a straight axle purchased from speedway motors. I do plan on racing it hopefully next year, that's what I built it for, not just for show. I love your website and keep up the good work.
Dustin Green
