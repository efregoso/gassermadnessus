~ Gary Wohlman's 33 Willys (Both of 'em) ~

 

Hi Byron,
I know this is huge and I hope you can receive it .
These cars belong to a long time , good friend of mine , Gary Wohlman , also from Loveland Co . With the help of his loving girlfriend, Ruthie , they have managed to put together these 2 cars . The purple and silver 33 is a steel car they found in Georgia about 7 years ago . As a project it was completely rebuilt and painted . It has a 400 SBC with 6/71 blower and 700R trans . Has a lot of Colorado miles on it , and believe me it runs strong .
The primered glass car was purchased 2 summers ago from Patrick Arron of Denver , a well known bodyman and Hemi man . Since these shots Gary has turned this racer into an awesome street legal ride . This bad bulldog has a 354 Hemi stroked to 427 with all the best parts , 727 and locked Dana 60 .
I had a ride in this car and it will smoke 'em till there's no road left .
Thanks to Gary , He's rekindled my boyhood dreams of gassers
I'll be sending pics and story of another friend's mile high 64 Chevy and my cars.
Bill Prall, Loveland Co
