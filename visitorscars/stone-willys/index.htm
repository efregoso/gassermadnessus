~ Steve Stone & Son's 40 Willys Sedan ~

Byron Just figured I would send a photo of my Willys. My son and I started on the 40 Willys when he was 17 we just finished it.  Big block Chevy turbo 400 12 bolt Chevy rear took us 3 years and lots of patience.  We built the entire car from the frame up it was at start a total junk. The 41 on the right is a true gasser 4 door small block auto with a Chevy rear I was told it was raced in the Carolinas with a hemi then here in Connecticut in the mid sixties.  If anyone has any info, we would appreciate it.  I am really proud of the 40 because all the body work and paint was done by my son. One of the few father and son projects that I know ever got finished.
Steve Stone
