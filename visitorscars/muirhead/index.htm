~ Ron Muirhead' 55 Chevy ~

Hi Byron, My name is Ron Muirhead. I sent you an email and a pic of me a couple of years ago. The pic was taken when I was about 6 or 7 years old beside Gary Becks Export 'A' car in Edmonton, Alberta, CANADA, around 1973/74. Just wanted to let you know how much I enjoy you're site. I cruise in every so often to look around, and when ever I need a kick in the ass to get back on my own project. The site just keeps getting better and better every time I'm here. After many years of drooling over my Uncles 55 Chevy, he finally decided that he probably wasn't going to ever get to it so he gave it to me, I tried to pay him something for it, but he wasn't having it, he just wants to see it back on the track. He built the car in 1969, got married in 1973 and the car sat in the same garage in the same spot up until a year ago when I took it. This is the same Uncle that took the picture of me beside Gary's car. As things progress I'll send you a pic or two if you would like, so you can see how things are coming along.  This is the only Picture of my Uncles car. It's from about 1971. The pic was taken in late spring when he was getting ready for the upcoming race season. The car behind is my Aunt's 69 340/4spd Dart that they bought new and still have today. Catch you later.
Thanks again,
Ron
