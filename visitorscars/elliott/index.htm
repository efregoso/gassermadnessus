~ Steve Elliott's 39 Fordson Gasser ~

These are a few pictures of Steve Elliott's 39 Fordson gasser. This car has a lot of hot rodding history. Steve has owned it since he was 16, and it became a hot rod a long time before that. When Steve first built it he was at school. It was running a 1700cc, red with flames, smoothys on rear & Wolfy slots on the front. It was also featured in Custom Car magazine. It is currently running a Rover V8, auto B&M shifter, Mustang rear axle, drop-tube front end. It will hopefully be fully finished in a couple of years time.
Ross Elliott
