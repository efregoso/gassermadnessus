Power is provided by a 392 blown Hemi from Big Al's Toy Box. Blower is a 6-71 with two Edelbrock carbs. 727 Torqueflite tranny It has a 9 inch Ford rearend complete with ladder bars. Absolutely street legal and enjoying every minute of it.
