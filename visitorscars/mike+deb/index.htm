~ Mike & Deb Novosel's 63 Falcon ~

Byron, After owing this car for over thirty years now it is back on the street. It was my brothers family car for several years and was totaled. I had started to make it into a gasser in the eighties and was sidelined on it for several years. I have been looking and picking up parts for the last 6 years. It has an Econoline front axle with disc brakes. A nine inch ford with 35 spline axles and 430 gears. It is powered by a 489 big block Chevy. The top was removed and I put a 12 point cage in. It is licensed for the street and has turned low 11s in the quarter.
Mike & Deb
