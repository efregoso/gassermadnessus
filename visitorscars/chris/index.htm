~ Chris' 57 Chevy ~

Hello Byron,
My name is Chris and I, along with several good friends, put this 70's style 57' 210 post car together in 9 months. The car has a built 350, 10.5:1 compression ratio, tunnel ram, 2 Holley's, 244/254 .510/.533 cam. The rear end is a 9" Ford with 4:88 gears, full spool, and Moser 31 spline axles.
We're running a Muncie M-21 with a Centerforce clutch. The car underwent a frame off restoration from 8/10/03 through 4/8/04 and has been terrorizing the streets of Upstate NY ever since.
