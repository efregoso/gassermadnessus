~ Neil Cawley's 65 Comet & 35 Plymouth ~

Hi Byron, My name is Neal Cawley, I live in Albuquerque, New Mexico. Big fan of Gasser Madness here….I’ve been on the website for a while. Here are some pics of a couple cars I’m building. The ’65 Comet is a Caliente converted to Cyclone badging and an F-1 straight axle, drilled and chromed. I have a 390 FE being built for it now. It has a four speed Toploader and a 9 inch rear.
The second car is a 35 Plymouth coupe. It’s chopped and the body, fenders and running boards are all welded together. It has a 331 Hemi with a La Salle three speed tranny. It runs a dual four intake now, but I have a 354 Hemi and 871 Blower ready for it now…..
Thank you, Neal Cawley
