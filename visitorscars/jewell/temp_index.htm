hi...I love your gasser madness page...the old race car pictures blow me away...I noticed you have a section on lost and found race cars...I have a sorry story for you...Bill "Grumpy' Jenkins raced a 55 Chevy early in his career...it was called the "Monster Mash"...in the early seventies I was offered a 55 Chevy for sale...the car was cheeseholed... {the frame and body were lightened by cutting away as much metal as possible} it turned out that the car was the original "Monster Mash" 55 Chevy ...I turned down the car...at the time I lived in a small town in northwestern NJ...the "Monster Mash" wound up getting crushed for junk...if you can possibly get a picture of the car while Jenkins raced it I would appreciate seeing it...meanwhile here is a picture of my 55 Chevy...which is unmolested and I have owned for thirty years...

Thanks in advance...Henry A Jewell, Great Meadows, NJ
