~ Ben Rushforth's Outlaw Anglia ~

Hi, 

Here are a few pics of my car.  It's been raced in the UK since the mid 70s and now has a 460 BBF and C6 and a 9inch Ford rear. It will be running mid 10s in the Outlaw Anglia championship this year.
The car was built in the mid 70s by the Flavell Bros. The car was saved from the junk yard! it was built and raced until 94 when it was parked until I bought the car in may 2001. Before I bought, it it had run many different motor from a Ford Zephyr V6 to a 454 BBC including Olds and Pontiac motors.   In the mid 80s, it gained its super gas frame which it retains today
