~ Miles Sherlock's 61 Falcon Gasser  ~

Hi there
My name is Miles Sherlock and I also belong to the Executioners car club of London, UK.
I thought you might like to see these photos of my 1961 Falcon gasser. It has a 302/c4/9inch combo, straight tube axle on parallel springs, 4x15 and 7x15 Cragar s/s wheels covered in 600x15 Silvertowns and cheater slicks on the rear, all topped off in metalflake green paint.
The car has taken three years to build and this year will see it's strip debut.
Hope you like it.
