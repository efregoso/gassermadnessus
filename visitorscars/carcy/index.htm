~ Jeff Carcy's 33 Willys ~

Here are some pictures of my ’33 Willys that I built. It started as a basic outer shell with no floor. I chopped it, stretched it, channeled it, and reinforced it with mostly steel and some wood.

Here are some specs:

3 ½” chop, 5” stretched doors, 7” stretched front.

392 Hemi loaded with a bunch of goodies, 671 blower, Nitrous

727 trans, Gear Vendors overdrive, Dana 60.

4 wheel disk brakes and Bilstein coil overs on all four corners.

This is a street driven car and does not fail to draw attention.

Jeff Carcy

Junction City, WI
