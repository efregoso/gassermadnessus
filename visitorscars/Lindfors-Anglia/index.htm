Update: 1/29/2009

Byron, I own the Blair's Speed Shop Anglia (Red & White) that is on the visitors ride page. Since I originally sent in the info we have found out more and some of what we were originally told is incorrect. I would appreciate it if you could update

The car was built at Blair's with Pete Eastwood doing most of the chassis work (not Chuck Finders as listed). It was owned by Jay Lindsley who I have had recent contact with and was able to get original photos from. We are putting it back the way it was with a 427 Hilborn injected Chevy and 4 speed Muncie. It will even be painted the same with the blessing of Don Blair and Phil Lukens. Hope to have it up and running this year by October.
Thanks, Don Lindfors
This car was run out of Blair's Speed Shop in the 60's & 70's. This is Irwindale Raceway circa 1971. I am currently restoring/ rebuilding it. Hilborn Blown 6-71 Blown 351 Cleveland in the original Chuck Finders chassis. I did replace the cage and the Corvair steering gave way to a Vega box. Oh yeah and I added front brakes!
Don Lindfors, Orange, CA
