~ Bill Boland's - Larry Teeter's 32 Ford Vicky ~

Byron, Here is a picture of Larry Teeters Vicky restored. The owner is Bill Boland, he is my cousins husband.  He restored the car as a street rod 22 plus years ago. He sold the car and just bought it back a couple of years ago. He thought it was gone forever but a friend saw it for sale on the internet in Southern California. I think it only had 1400 more miles on it than when he sold it.
