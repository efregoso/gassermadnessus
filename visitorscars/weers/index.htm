~ Clarence Weers' 57 Chevy C/A ~

Here are some shots of my 57 which use to campaign as a c/altered back in the late 60s. I picked up the car in the early 70s and have had it in storage ever since. I plan on putting it together for the street and a little nostalgia racing. The car has an Econoline axle, fiberglass nose, 27% setback of the engine. It has a 67 Vette 327 rock crusher 4 speed with a 12 bolt rear.
