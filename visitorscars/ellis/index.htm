~ Ron Ellis' 56 Chevy ~

Hello I was admiring your web site that my son made me aware of. I am a member of www.classicgassers.com I have been running the nostalgia circuit here in Florida for about 14 years. I have owned my 56 for 22 years and had a lot of fun building and racing. I have been building cars since 1968. I also teach auto shop here in Ft. Myers, Florida. If you would like to put the picture of my car on your web site I would be happy to see it there. I run a small block with mufflers and street tires. Never any nitrous. I wish there was more nostalgia action down here. Keep up the good work.  Ron Ellis
