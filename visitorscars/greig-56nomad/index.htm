Howdy Byron,
Here's some photos of my 56, I bought it in 1976, daily driver for two years with the Econoline axle, 327, Muncie, orange & white tuck & roll, it drove & handled great, then I had the idea of a "quickie fluff & buff". I started the frame off in 1978, its now 2008 & still in the garage minus the axle & wheel wells. Still a small block/four speed car, Ansen Ground Grabbers, Old Torque Thrusts. Planning on paint late this summer & hopefully back on the road in mid-2009 for the CHRR. I really appreciate Gasser Madness, it’s always a great way to start the day.
