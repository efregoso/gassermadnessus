~ Chris Baltimore's 64 Falcon Sprint ~

Here's my baby, a 1964 falcon Sprint (mildly modified). I'm 21 years old, and on a budget. I wanted to build something different, something no one has anymore, a street legal gasser. It's not all period correct, but I don't care, I love it. She has a 30 over 302, home made headers, and 40" ladder bars, 8 point cage, among many other mod's.
It's not done ,but as I said, I'm on a budget. I'd appreciate any comments and/or suggestions for my project. I didn't grow up with these types of race cars, all I have are books and the web for ideas. It's not perfect, bit it's not an import either.
