~ Byron's Gasser Madness ~

~ Scott Hasko's 41 Willys  ~

Hi Byron,  I thought I would show you a few photos just taken of my coupe. This was a 3 year project and I'm still finishing it off. It's all steel and although it's low tech I can honestly say that 95% of it was completed in my garage working solo. I even shot the paint in the back yard. It has a small block and the combination should get me into the 11's easily. Not the fastest thing out there, but fast enough for me right now.
Your site started out as an inspiration for me to build this car as they looked in the old days, and then it became one of my best sources for documentation so I could get ideas. I was lucky to find an uncut car so creating a gasser out of it required me to start from scratch. I have purchased many parts off of your classifieds, and sold just as many too. Plus I was able to make a lot of contacts which helped me tremendously, and I have been able to "repay" those favors by giving advice to some of the folks I met through your site. Cool Stuff! 
I'm still looking in on the site every day, but wanted to say thanks for the hard work I'm sure must be required to manage a site like the Gasser Madness. Attached are some shots of the fruits of my labors. I hope you like the way I've done the car. Look at 'em and toss 'em out, use them, whatever. I could write a book on all I had to do to build this thing and all I had to learn in the process, but I don't want to bore you. Suffice to say I think I'm going to find that I enjoyed the buildup more than I will the driving. Man, did I have some fun...
-Scott Hasko
