~ Bill Schin's 63 Chevy Nova ~

First Look   October 2008 Update

I'm a big fan of gassers and of your site. I'm from central New Jersey, and here are a few pictures of my 1963 nova that I'm in the process of building. My 23yr old son installed new quarters, door skins and fenders. Finished off the rest of the body work and painted the car. I mini tubbed it and installed a 9 inch rear. I hand fabricated the front sub chassis and had a Speedway straight axle set chromed. I'm having a big block Chevy motor built, and it will have a 400 turbo trans. Will send more pictures when completed. Thanks...Bill Schin
