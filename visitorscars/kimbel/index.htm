~ Monty Kimbel's 53 Willys ~

I'd like to say this is a great web site keep up the good work ! I've had several race cars and I always wanted a nostalgia race car . I run across this  53 Willys in a wrecking yard in OK.  It was going to be crushed . I paid $300.00 for it and I plan on putting it back in it's racing condition! I have found the grandson of the man that built an raced it.  He said it had a small block Chevy and a four speed trans . I also bought a 47 Ford two ton truck to restore to pull it with . I hope to make some of the nostalgia shows and races with  them .

Monty Kimbell
