~ Pete Arnell's 55 Chevy ~

Hey Byron. Here is my 55 from western Canada. Thought it might make your visitor's rides page. Small block with aluminum Edelbrock heads, demon carb, Muncie 4 speed. Car has '57 olds posi, ladder bars , rear seat removed with half cage. Car was originally from Spokane Washington, original owner had it for 44 years and ran a shop called Big Dyno Motive. This was a street car that never saw a racetrack. Originally blue with a stroked 283. Thanks, Pete Arnell. Alberta Canada.
