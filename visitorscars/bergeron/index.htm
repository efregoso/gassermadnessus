HI BYRON

 MY NAME IS DAN BERGERON.  MY CAR IS A 62 CHEVY II . MY BROTHER GOT IT IN 1970 AND BROKE A ROD IN 1972 AND IT SAT IN THE BACK YARD.  I WORKED ON IT PUTTING A STRAIGHT AXLE IN IT AND MADE 55 INCH LADDER BARS FOR IT, MADE THE NOSE TILT. ALL DONE IN HIGH SCHOOL.  IN 1981 I GOT MY LICENSE AND DROVE IT TO SCHOOL IN BLACK PRIMER WITH A 327 2-4'S TUNNELRAM, 4 SPEED AND A 12 BOLT.  NOW ITS GOT A 454 WITH A AUTO AND A DANA 60.
