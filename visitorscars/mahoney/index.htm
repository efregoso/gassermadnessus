~ Mike Mahoney's 38 Plymouth ~

Hi Byron,
I attached some photos of my car. It is a 1938 Plymouth Business Coupe. When I got it, the frame had been butchered and there was no floor, engine etc. I have replaced the floors, tubbed the rear fenders, set back the firewall 6 inches to clear the 440 Mopar I am putting in it. I plan to put a tunnel ram intake on for now since I can't afford a blower set up. It has a Art Morrison perimeter frame and I built the crossmembers and mounts for the engine and transmission. It has taken a lot of years to build, I have used it as a stress reliever for my job. Keep up the great job with the web site.
Take care,
Mike Mahoney
