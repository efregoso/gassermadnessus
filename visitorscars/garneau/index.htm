Here's a few pics of Riff Raff.
Riff Raff was scratch built and is driven by the owner, Gasser Dave. It was featured in the March, '06 issue of Drag Racing Action magazine and was profiled on Inside Drag Racing, airing on ESPN ll.
Sporting a warmed over 350 cu. in. small block, four-speed, and 6.00:1 9"rear, it is raced on a weekly basis, touring the Southeast with the Classic Gear Jammers all stick club. You'll find none of the new gadgets here. No electronics or NOS, the foot-activated clutch is DUMPED at 8000 rpm, which makes for some awesome 1st, 2nd, and 3rd gear wheelstands, and the clutch style four-speed is worked via an old time 'H' pattern shifter. And the best part is, Ol' Riff Raff wins races!
