~ Mazmanian's "Football" A/GS Austin ~

 
 

Here's one I thought deserved it's own page...The "Football" car of "Big John" Mazmanian.  I've been waiting for years to see this car restored.  Tom Willford had the body and collected several pieces, then sold it to Mike Duffy, who subsequently sold it to Ralph Whitworth.  Ralph had a new chassis constructed for it since the original had been lost over the years after it landed under KS Pittman's Opel and finally disappeared.  All I can say is that Ralph did a beautiful job on the reconstruction!  Thanks Ralph!
