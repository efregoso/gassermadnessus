Pete Garramone ~ Colorado (mostly) Drag Racing

I've known Pete for several years now, even though we've only met a few times at previous California Hot Rod Reunions. We first met online back in the days of the NHRA Newsgroup before there were a number of Nostalgia email groups (Gas-FX, Standard 1320, DRI, etc.) or websites (Gasser Madness, We Did it for Love, Standard 1320, etc.). Pete was having trouble posting photos and asked me for help. While Pete is more of a "wire wheel" guy, he had a lot of great Gasser photos too. I have posted many of them here on Gasser Madness, but, for whatever reason, never put up a Pete Garramone page. This page remedies that situation with some very cool photos. Thanks Pete!

If you'd like to purchase any of Pete's photos, you can go to Pete's website at www.dragpixbypete.com and order directly from him.

By the way, you should consider yourself warned that all of Pete's photos are registered with the Copyright office and he is adamant about pursuing copyright violators, so if you want to download for your own computer, have at it, but if you put them up on your own website or try to sell them, you do so at your own risk.
