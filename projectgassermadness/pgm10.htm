~ Part 10 ~

~ Final Fab ~

Byron, Project Gasser Madness is moving forward. Troy Stacy of S&S Fabrication and Headers in Pumpkin Center, CA has been doing final fabrication which included: headers, update on roll cage, aluminum firewall, floor, and door panels, gas tank, and battery box.

The motor and trans are in the car but will come out when it's taken to Art Foster Engines in Santa Maria. Art and his son A. J. will complete the mechanics of the car: parachute, windows, gas pedal, plus many of the small but necessary items needed to build a race car.
