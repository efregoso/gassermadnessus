Project Gasser Madness!

 

Several weeks ago, I received an email from Dave Miller of Lompoc, CA.  It it, he asked if I would be interested in a "Project Gasser Madness" Willys.  I replied that it would be cool, but I didn't have the funds to help out with a deal like that.  Fortunately (for me, anyway), Dave wasn't after dollars, he just wanted to know if I'd be interested in "documenting" the build of a Gasser.  I don't remember my exact reply, but it was something along the lines of "Hell yes!"

At any rate, Here's "Project Gasser Madness!"

Byron (22 October 2002)
